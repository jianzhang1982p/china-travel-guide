## 🎨 How to Capture

### **Method 1: iPhone Screenshots**
1. Open the screen you want to capture
2. Press Side Button + Volume Up (or Home button for older iPhones)
3. Crop if needed
4. Save to Photos

### **Method 2: Screen Recording (Recommended)**
1. Open Control Center
2. Tap Screen Recording button
3. Complete the entire flow
4. Stop recording
5. Extract individual screenshots from video

### **Method 3: Android**
1. Open the screen
2. Press Power + Volume Down
3. Or use "Screenshot" in quick settings
4. Save to Gallery

---

## 📝 Tips for Good Screenshots

1. **Use good lighting** - Natural light is best
2. **Clean your screen** - Wipe fingerprints before capturing
3. **Use airplane mode** - Prevents notifications from appearing
4. **Enable "Do Not Disturb"** - No popups during capture
5. **Use dark mode** - If available, looks more professional
6. **Crop unnecessary parts** - Remove status bar if not needed
7. **Add annotations** - Use arrows/boxes to highlight important elements
